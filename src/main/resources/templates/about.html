Our Story

Inventory Now is built and maintained by a small team of developers in Charleston, SC. It was originally created in 2020 as a lightweight tool for ticket brokers to manage their inventory. Since we started, its been great to see so many people use the app to do SO much more! Recently, after years of gathering support and feedback, we completely redesigned and rewrote the entire app in order to create a platform that would allow us to scale and add all of the features that you told us you wanted. Today, we are delivering the most robust, most customizable and (most importantly) the most simple solution available. We strongly believe in the power of simplicity through design and work tirelessly to ensure every feature we create meets our high standards.
